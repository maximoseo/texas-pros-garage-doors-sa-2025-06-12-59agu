Common Garage Door Problems and Solutions

Published on June 12, 2025

Garage doors, while essential to our homes, can experience various mechanical issues over time. Common problems include doors that won't open or close properly due to dead batteries, misaligned sensors, or broken springs. Unusual noises during operation often signal loose hardware or lack of lubrication, while unexpected reversals typically stem from sensor issues. Regular maintenance, including monthly inspections and proper lubrication, can prevent many problems. However, certain repairs like spring replacements and cable fixes should always be handled by professionals. Understanding these issues helps homeowners maintain their garage doors effectively and recognize when expert help is needed.
