Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical garage door installation take?

A standard garage door installation typically takes 4-6 hours for residential properties. However, custom installations or complex setups might require additional time. We'll provide a specific timeline during your consultation.

What warranty coverage do you offer?

We provide a lifetime warranty on all installation work, plus manufacturer warranties on parts and materials (typically 10-15 years). Our service repairs are guaranteed for 90 days.

How often should I schedule garage door maintenance?

We recommend professional maintenance every 6-12 months to ensure optimal performance and safety. Regular maintenance can prevent costly repairs and extend your door's lifespan.

Do you offer emergency repair services?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency repair services with a 30-minute response time guarantee within the San Antonio metro area. Additional fees may apply for after-hours service.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, cash, checks, and offer flexible financing options. We also provide special discounts for military, seniors, and first responders.
